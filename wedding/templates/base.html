Getting There
Manila

Arrive in Manila (IATA code: MNL) on Saturday, Feb 13. Fly out a day after the church wedding on Saturday night, Feb 20. View flights to Manila »



If you are connecting directly to Caticlan, allow for 3 hours of transit time in Manila. You need to take a taxi from one terminal to the next.



We have reserved rooms from Feb 18 – 20 at Makati Shangri-la, wedding reception and after-party location. Superior rooms cost 11,647 pesos per night (~$250), including breakfast, Wi-Fi, and taxes.

Boracay

Fly out of Manila and into Caticlan (IATA code: MPH) on Sunday morning, Feb 14. View flights » Fly back to Manila on Thursday early afternoon, Feb 18. View flights »


We have reserved rooms at 3 hotels from Feb 14 - 18: Asya, the wedding location; Discovery Shores, pre- and post-wedding dinner location; and Villa Caemilla, a 15-minute walk away from Asya.


Asya rooms start at 17,100 pesos per night (~$375). Discovery Shores rooms start at 15,200 (~$315). Villa Caemilla rooms start at 7,800 (~$170). Prices include round-trip land and boat transfers, breakfast, Wi-Fi, and taxes.
