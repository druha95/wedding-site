Getting There
Manila

Book an international flight that arrives in Manila(IATA code MNL) on Saturday, February 13, and departs a day after the church wedding on Saturday night, February 20. View flights to Manila »


If you are connecting directly to Caticlan, allow for 3 hours of transit time in Manila. You need to take a taxi from one terminal to the next


We have reserved rooms from Feb 18 - 20 at Makati Shangri-la, wedding reception and after-party location. Superior rooms cast 11,647 pesos per night(~$250), including breakfast, Wi-Fi, and taxes

Boracay

Book a domestic flight out of Manila and into Caticlan (IATA code MPH) on sunday morning, Feb 14. View flights » Fly back to Manila on Thursday early afternoon, Feb 18 View flights »


We have reserved rooms at two hotels from Feb 14 - 18 Asya, the wedding location, and Villa Caemilla, a 15-minute walk away.


Asay rooms start at 17,100 pesos per night (~$375). Villa Cemilla rooms start at 7,800 pesos per night (~$170). Prices include round-trip land and boat transfers from Caticlan, breakfast, Wi-Fi, and taxes.
